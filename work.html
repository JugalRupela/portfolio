What I've been upto.

Here's a selection of some recent work. For more information on my previous work feel free to contact me.

INTERACTIVE GAMES

Interactive games using any normal 2d camera/webcams, character moves and jumps when the user moves and raise hand respectively.

TOUCH WALL

Make any wall touch with projection mapping, all we need is a blank white wall with a graphic outline to map the projection and make it touch.

INTERACTIVE JUMP DISPENSER

Electronically assembled floormat where user has to keep jumping to get certain points, once it reaches 100% it will dispense out a free drink.

VIRTUAL MAKEUP

Created and deployed a prototype for testing beauty products virtually such as lipstick, blusher, eyeliner, etc.

VIRTUAL WARDROBE PHOTO BOOTH

Try different japanese outfits and snap a picture of yourself in front of this virtual mirror.

FACEBOOK AR

Developed an Augmented Reality application which can be scanned from your facebook app, so you dont have to download a seperate application.

MAKE ANY WALL TOUCH

Developed and Integrated sensors to make any wall touch, all you need is blank wall.

WHEEL OF FORTUNE

A wheel of fortune where user simply touch to spin the wheel and try their luck.
